33040. Algebra I. (9, 10, 11, 12).

Credits: 1

Length: Year

NCAA Approved: Yes

Elective: No

Prerequisites: N/A

Fee: 0

Description: In Algebra I the student develops an understanding of the basic structure and language of Algebra; a tool used to represent and solve a variety of real-world situations. The student uses tables, models, and graphs to interpret algebraic expressions, equations, and inequalities to analyze functions. In addition, the student uses calculators, computers, and graphing utilities in problem solving. Other areas of study are global processes, geometry, and data analysis and probability. Literacy strategies are integrated throughout the curriculum.
